The Best Movies According to Mark (CodeMacLife.com)

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

Terminator 2: Judgement Day

It's Terminator! Arnie is a legend and the movie was ahead of time and predicts what we are falling into with AI.
Trailer (YouTube)

CODA

CODA (Child of Dead Adults) is a beautifully put together movie about a teenager who attempts to help her family's fishing business. Ruby does this while pursuing her dream of becoming a singer. [AppleTV+ exclusive]
Trailer (YouTube)

Forgetting Sarah Marshall

One of the movies from the rom-com genre I actually like. The cast is perfect and the setting in Hawaii brings the movie to life. Did I mention the music is great?
